How I became a web developer

During my journey to becoming a web developer, I went through a variety of industries, accumulating a wealth of experiences that shaped my eclectic skill set In my tender 20s, I had already delved into the physically demanding world of brick masonry, honed my construction prowess, navigated the fast-paced environment of a line cook, and delved into the intricacies of mechanics, And my passion for all things tech remained unwavering. The constant exposure to different fields sparked a passion in me and made me realize that my heart belongs in the digital field. Driven by a natural love of technology, I explored the web development path, eager to integrate my skills into a cohesive set of dynamic knowledge that could traverse the virtual landscape. This transformation lies not only in professional development but also in a personal journey fueled by an enduring fascination with the limitless possibilities of the digital world.
